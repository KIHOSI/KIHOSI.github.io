Tonopoly: A Monopoly-like Board Game with Toio

UIST 2021 SIC
Chi-Huan Chiang, Ching-Wen Hung


We present Tonopoly, a monopoly-like board game using Toio robots. Tonopoly is a 2-4 player board game. Each player becomes planet explorer and needs to collect resources to win the game. There are many islands in the planet. Players choose Toio robots as their spaceships and double-tap them to move on islands. When the explorers arrive at the same island, they start a battle at the center to fight for resource. Explorers pick up and shake their spaceships to charge up an attack, and robots attack to their opponent. The more intensely they shakes, the more damage the attack deals to their opponent. Red/Green/Yellow island gives explorers power-ups that increase their ATK/HP/Lcuk, which helps them win battles to get rubies. If an explorer visits the brown island, it can't leave the island for one round. If the explorer tries to escape the island, it will be arrested and sent back. Finally, the explorer that collects three rebies monopolizes the resources on the planet.
