Ching-Wen Hung | 洪靖雯 (HOSI)

Ching-Wen Hung is a Ph.D. candidate from Interactive Graphics Lab and Communication and Multimedia Lab at the Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia, National Taiwan University, advised by Bing-Yu Chen. Her current research interests focus on haptics, virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), novel interaction and perception in the human-computer interaction (HCI) field.
